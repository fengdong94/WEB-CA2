About us

imagem 1

It all started as a dream. A dream, not only full of cream and sugar — but one like a real, real dream. Like one born inside of our Grandmother's heart. My Grandma was born to bake, she spent years of her life creating and improving recipes that delighted both eyes and taste of anyone that would try it. I grew up with the sweet cinnamon smell on every Sunday afternoon — with the sound of the mixer my grandmother's love pouring into the batter — more love than flour. When her health asked for a break, we decided to carry on with her legacy : following her recipes, layer by layer, with love of a Granny and a spoon in hand. Today, Cakezilla is our way to keep our Grandma's memory alive, with a pinch of nostalgia and joy in slices. Because here, cake is never just a cake. It is a hug, it is a celebration, it is legacy and it is all about Grandma. From our Kitchen to your heart! Cakezilla team
